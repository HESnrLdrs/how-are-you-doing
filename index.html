How Are You Doing?

When life throws you a major disruption, it helps to pause and take stock

Serious illness, family crisis, relationship breakdown, job loss – when something like this hits, it's easy to feel overwhelmed and lose your bearings.

This short questionnaire helps you get a clearer picture of where you are right now across three areas:

Coping: How you're managing emotionally
Practical: How you're handling day-to-day life
Self: How connected you feel to who you are

It takes about 10-15 minutes.

Everything stays private. Your responses live only on your device – nothing is stored or shared anywhere.
