How Are You Doing?

A self-assessment for navigating major life disruptions

When life throws you a major disruption - serious illness, family crisis, relationship breakdown, or job loss - it's easy to feel overwhelmed.

This brief assessment helps you understand where you are across three key areas:

Coping: How you're managing emotionally and psychologically
Practical: How you're handling daily life and logistics
Self: How you're maintaining your sense of identity and purpose

Time required: 10-15 minutes

Your privacy: All responses stay on your device. Nothing is stored or shared.
